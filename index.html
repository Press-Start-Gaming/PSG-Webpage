About Press Start Gaming

Server Specifications

Our dedicated server CPU runs on an Intel Core i9 12900K CPU with 64GB DDR5 RAM. The server is housed in a secure datacenter located in Dallas, TX. Looking for dependable and affordable server hosting? We would like to work with you. Contact us via our Discord today.

Support PSG

Press Start Gaming is 100% community funded. Visit our Patreon to become a supporter. You can also support us by shopping at our merch store, becoming a Subscriber on Discord, or by making a one-time donation on PayPal. All proceeds from donations and purchases are put directly back into the community and we release periodic financial reports on our Discord for transparency.

Community Events

We host lots of social gaming events, many of which are streamed on our Twitch channel. You are invited to join us for Jackbox, Gartic Phone, Discord Activities, Geoguessr, trivia night, tabletop games, multplayer RTS lobbies, custom operations in Squad and other games, learn to play sessions, and more.
